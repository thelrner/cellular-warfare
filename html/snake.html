Snake Wars

Score

Rules

Regular snake rules apply: eat apples, avoid the wall and yourself. Enemy cells spawn randomly. Eating an apple spawns friendly cells. Cell propagation obeys Conway's Game with a twist -- friendly cells capture enemy cells by surrounding them.

Consuming an apple earns 50 points. Capturing an enemy cell earns 1 point.

Controls

  • SPACE - New Game
  • UP/DOWN/LEFT/RIGHT ARROWS - Move
  • P - Pause
